TeenChatTown Chat Rules

TeenChatTown is a community for respectful, safe, and enjoyable conversations. By using the platform, you agree to follow these rules.

1

Respect Everyone

Treat all users with respect. Harassment, bullying, personal attacks, threats, discrimination, or hate speech are not allowed.

2

Keep the Chat Appropriate

Do not post sexual, graphic, violent, or disturbing content. Any sexual content involving minors will result in an immediate permanent ban and may be reported.

3

Protect Personal Information

Do not share your address, phone number, passwords, financial information, private photographs, or other sensitive details. Do not expose another user’s private information without permission.

4

No Predatory or Suspicious Behaviour

Grooming, sexual requests, manipulation, stalking, blackmail, or attempts to exploit another user are strictly prohibited. Report suspicious behaviour immediately.

5

No Spam or Disruption

Do not flood the chat, repeatedly send the same message, abuse capital letters, post excessive links, advertise without permission, or deliberately disturb conversations.

6

No Impersonation

Do not pretend to be another user, staff member, celebrity, organisation, or official representative. Misleading usernames and profiles may be removed.

7

Follow Staff Instructions

Moderators and administrators may warn, mute, kick, restrict, or ban users who violate the rules. Do not argue about moderation actions in public rooms. Use the official support or appeal system instead.

8

Do Not Evade Punishments

Creating alternate accounts or using technical methods to bypass a mute, ban, restriction, or account suspension is prohibited and may result in a permanent ban.

9

Report Problems Responsibly

Use the report feature for rule violations, harmful content, suspicious users, or security issues. False reports and misuse of the reporting system may lead to action against your account.

10

Community Safety

Any behaviour that harms users, disrupts the community, or misuses the platform may result in moderation action, even if it is not specifically listed above.

Enforcement

Rule violations may result in a warning, message removal, mute, kick, temporary suspension, account restriction, or permanent ban depending on severity and previous behaviour.

Serious safety concerns may be reported to the appropriate authorities.

By continuing to use TeenChatTown, you confirm that you understand and agree to follow these rules.
